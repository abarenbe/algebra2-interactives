Section 4.3: Dividing Polynomials

Master the essential skill of dividing polynomials using two powerful methods. Polynomial long division works for any divisor, while synthetic division offers a streamlined shortcut when dividing by linear factors. Both methods are fundamental for factoring higher-degree polynomials, finding roots, and simplifying rational expressions.

Recommended Learning Path

  1. Start with Polynomial Long Division to learn the general method
  2. Move to Synthetic Division for a faster approach with linear divisors
