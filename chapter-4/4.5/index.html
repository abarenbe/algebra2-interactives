Section 4.5: Solving Polynomial Equations

Master the techniques for finding solutions to polynomial equations of all degrees. This section builds on your factoring skills and introduces powerful theorems that help predict and find rational, irrational, and complex roots. You'll learn systematic methods to solve equations that can't be factored by inspection, including using the Rational Root Theorem and understanding how conjugate pairs of roots work together.

Recommended Learning Path

  1. Start with Factoring to Solve to build on your factoring skills
  2. Move to Rational Root Theorem for finding possible rational solutions
  3. Learn about Irrational Conjugates to understand radical roots
  4. Complete with Complex Conjugates for imaginary solutions
