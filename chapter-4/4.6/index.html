Section 4.6: The Fundamental Theorem of Algebra

The Fundamental Theorem of Algebra is one of the most important results in mathematics. It tells us exactly how many roots a polynomial has and provides powerful tools for finding those roots. Learn to write polynomials from their zeros and use Descartes' Rule to predict the nature of roots before solving.

Recommended Learning Path

  1. Start with The Fundamental Theorem to understand root counting
  2. Learn to Write Polynomials from Zeros working backwards
  3. Use Descartes' Rule of Signs to predict root types

The Fundamental Theorem

Every polynomial of degree n has exactly n roots (counting multiplicity and complex roots).
