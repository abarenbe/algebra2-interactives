Section 4.9

Modeling with Polynomial Functions

Welcome to Polynomial Modeling

Polynomial functions are powerful tools for modeling real-world phenomena. From the path of a projectile to the growth of populations, from business revenue models to engineering designs, polynomials help us understand and predict patterns in data.

In this section, you'll learn how to analyze data to determine what degree polynomial best fits it, how to create polynomial models from data points, and how to apply these models to solve practical problems. You'll work with real-world scenarios involving physics, business, and optimization.

Recommended Learning Path

  1. Start with Finite Differences - Learn how to analyze data patterns to determine polynomial degree
  2. Move to Polynomial Regression - Discover how to fit polynomial curves to data points
  3. Apply to Real-World Problems - Use polynomial models to solve practical applications
