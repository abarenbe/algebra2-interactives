Understanding Multiplicity

What is Multiplicity?

The multiplicity of a zero is the number of times that factor appears. It determines how the graph behaves at that x-intercept.

Odd Multiplicity (1, 3, 5...)

Graph crosses the x-axis

Higher odd = flatter crossing

Even Multiplicity (2, 4, 6...)

Graph touches and bounces back

Higher even = flatter touch
